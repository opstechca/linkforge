# Support

## Getting Help

For questions, bugs, or feature requests related to Bulk Issue Link Manager for Jira, contact us at [support@opstech.ca](mailto:support@opstech.ca).

We aim to respond within 1-2 business days.

## Common Issues

**The "Link Issues in Bulk" option does not appear in the menu**
- Ensure the app is installed and enabled on your Jira site.
- The option appears under the "..." (More actions) menu on any issue view.

**Some issues failed to link**
- The app shows failed issues in red after processing. Common reasons include insufficient permissions on the target issue or the link already exists in the opposite direction.
- You can copy the failed keys and retry.

**JQL query returns no results**
- Verify your JQL syntax is valid using Jira's built-in JQL search first.
- The app uses the same JQL engine as Jira search.

**The Link Summary panel is not visible**
- The panel may be collapsed. Look for the "Links" label in the issue's right sidebar.
- If it does not appear at all, check that the app is installed and you have read access to the issue's linked issues.
